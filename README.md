# Driver-Based FP&A Forecasting Model – Infosys Ltd.

## Overview

This project demonstrates the development of an integrated FP&A (Financial Planning & Analysis) forecasting model for Infosys Ltd. using Microsoft Excel. The model combines historical financial analysis, revenue forecasting, workforce planning, cost driver forecasting, scenario analysis, working capital forecasting, and cash flow forecasting to support strategic financial decision-making.

The objective was to replicate how FP&A teams transform historical company performance into forward-looking business plans and management insights.

---

## Business Objective

To build a driver-based financial forecasting model capable of projecting:

* Revenue Growth
* Operating Profitability
* Net Profitability
* Workforce Requirements
* Operating Costs
* Working Capital Requirements
* Free Cash Flow Generation
* Future Cash Position

The model was designed to support management decision-making under multiple business scenarios.

## Project Components

### 1. Historical Financial Analysis

Analyzed five years of historical performance to identify:

* Revenue Trends
* Operating Margin Trends
* Cost Structure Trends
* Workforce Trends
* Business Performance Patterns

---

### 2. Revenue Forecast Engine

Forecasted future revenue using scenario-driven assumptions.

Scenarios Included:

* Base Case
* Growth Scenario
* Recession Scenario
* Wage Inflation Scenario
* Hiring Expansion Scenario
* Margin Protection Scenario

---

### 3. Workforce Planning Model

Developed a workforce forecasting framework including:

* Historical Employee Analysis
* Employee Growth Trends
* Attrition Analysis
* Hiring Forecasts
* Workforce Capacity Planning
* Workforce Risk Assessment

Key Metrics:

* Employee Count
* Hiring Rate
* Attrition Rate
* Revenue Per Employee

---

### 4. Cost Forecasting Model

Built a cost-driver framework based on historical operating cost trends.

Cost Drivers:

* Employee Cost
* Technology Cost
* Administrative Cost
* Infrastructure Cost

Historical analysis indicated operating costs averaged approximately 75% of revenue.

---

### 5. Scenario Analysis Engine

Created a dynamic scenario engine allowing users to evaluate financial performance under multiple business conditions.

Forecast Drivers:

* Revenue Growth Rate
* Operating Margin
* Net Margin

---

### 6. Integrated Financial Forecast Model

Developed an integrated forecasting framework linking:

Revenue Forecast

↓

Workforce Forecast

↓

Cost Forecast

↓

Operating Profit

↓

Net Profit

↓

Working Capital Forecast

↓

Cash Flow Forecast

---

### 7. Working Capital Forecast

Forecasted:

* Trade Receivables
* Trade Payables
* Net Working Capital

Assumptions Used:

* DSO (Days Sales Outstanding)
* DPO (Days Payable Outstanding)

---

### 8. Cash Flow Forecast

Forecasted:

* Operating Cash Flow
* Capital Expenditure
* Free Cash Flow
* Ending Cash Balance

This enabled evaluation of future liquidity and cash generation capacity.
